1. Tapenade(Olive Spread)

    2. This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a hit at parties. My father recomends:

    "Make this the night before so that the flavours have time to blend. Just bring it up to the room temperature before you serve it. In the winter,try serving it warm."

  2. Ingredients

    • 80z. jar sundried tomatoes
    • large garlic cloves
    • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
    • 1 t. capers

  3. Instructions

    4. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until a smooth as possible.

    Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.

    Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish(optional).
